About The Hackathon

eSamudaay is excited to organize a 2-day hackathon in collaboration with Changepay and Ceedi. At the start of the hackathon, students will be given challenges based on Open Network for Digital Commerce (ONDC). By end of the hackathon, students will be expected to submit working MVP.

Participants can attend the hackathon in person as well as digitally. The venue for in-person attendance is Manipal Institute of Technology, Manipal, Karnataka and link for online presence is Google meet

Rules :-

  • The hackathon is open to 2nd, 3rd, and final year engineering students of all branches.
  • Students can participate alone or with a team. The maximum team size is 6 members. Students are encouraged to participate in teams.
  • The deadline for registration is 26th July, 9 PM.
  • The problem statements for the hackathon will be given at 11 AM on 29th July.4. The deadline for submission of the solution will be 8 PM 30th July.
  • The winners will be declared on 31st July, 10 PM.
